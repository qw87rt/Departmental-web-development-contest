The Institution

Saint Joseph College is a Catholic school located in Tunga-tunga Maasin City, Southern Leyte. It was established on February 20, 1928 and currently headed by the school president, His Exellency Most Rev. Precioso D. Cantillas, SDB, D.D. The insitution is known for its primary purpose of Catholic education and also quality academic training.

The Department

The College of Computer Studies has already endured and thrived for many years in Saint Joseph College. It was introduced to the school in 1994 and is currently headed by its departmental dean, Riza Lynn O. Siega, MCS. The department is based on the fourth floor of the main campus, where computer labs and other facilities are situated to cater to the needs of the students' learning. It currently offers three undergraduate courses.
